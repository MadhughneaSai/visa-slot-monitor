# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 2024

## Overview

Visa Slot Monitor is a Chrome extension that helps users monitor visa slot availability. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Email Address:** Only if you choose to enter it for notifications
- **Extension Settings:** Your mode/phase preferences (stored locally)

### What We DON'T Collect
- Personal information
- Browsing history
- Passwords or credentials
- Any data from other websites

## Data Storage

- All data is stored **locally** on your device using Chrome's storage API
- Email addresses are only used to send you alerts via EmailJS
- No data is sold or shared with third parties

## Third-Party Services

- **EmailJS:** Used to send email notifications. Subject to [EmailJS Privacy Policy](https://www.emailjs.com/legal/privacy-policy/)
- **checkvisaslots.com:** The extension reads publicly available data from this website

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| storage | Save your settings locally |
| notifications | Show desktop alerts |
| tabs | Open monitoring tab |
| alarms | Schedule periodic checks |

## Data Security

- No sensitive data is transmitted
- All communication with EmailJS is encrypted (HTTPS)
- No server-side storage of user data

## Your Rights

You can:
- Clear all stored data by removing the extension
- Choose not to provide an email address
- Disable notifications at any time

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, please open an issue on GitHub.
